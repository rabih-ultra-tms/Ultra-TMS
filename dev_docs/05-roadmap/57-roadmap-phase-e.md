## Phase Overview

Phase E completes the platform with specialty logistics verticals (household goods, final mile, auto transport, bulk/tanker) and introduces a capacity marketplace connecting shippers directly with carriers.

### Key Objectives

1. **Household Goods** - Moving industry support
2. **Final Mile** - Last-mile delivery
3. **Auto Transport** - Vehicle shipping
4. **Bulk/Tanker** - Specialized commodities
5. **Marketplace** - Shipper-carrier matching
