## Description
Factoring analytics report showing total factored volume, factoring fees, carrier quick-pay utilization, factoring company performance, and cost analysis (factoring fees vs direct payment savings).

## Key Design Considerations
- Cost-benefit analysis of factoring program helps evaluate whether factoring relationships are profitable
- Carrier-level factoring usage identifies opportunities to convert carriers to direct payment terms
