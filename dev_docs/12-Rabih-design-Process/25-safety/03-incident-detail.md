## Description
Detailed incident record showing full incident information including description, photos, involved parties, vehicle damage, injuries, witness statements, investigation notes, corrective actions, and resolution status.

## Key Design Considerations
- Timeline view of incident from initial report through investigation to resolution
- Photo/document gallery with annotation capabilities for evidence management

## Dependencies
- Incidents List for navigation
- Document service for photo/file attachments
- Driver and Equipment services for involved party details
