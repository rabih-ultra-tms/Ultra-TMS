## Description
Email delivery management interface showing email send volumes, delivery rates, bounce rates, spam complaint rates, email templates, and provider configuration for platform-wide email communications.

## Key Design Considerations
- Email delivery health dashboard with bounce and spam complaint monitoring against provider thresholds
- Email template management with preview, version history, and per-tenant customization capabilities

## Dependencies
- Email service provider APIs (SendGrid, SES, etc.)
- Communication service for email sending
- Notification service for email-based notifications
