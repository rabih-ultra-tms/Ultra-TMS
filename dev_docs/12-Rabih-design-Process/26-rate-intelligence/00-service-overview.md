# Service 26: Rate Intelligence

## Service Overview
The Rate Intelligence service provides market rate data, historical rate analysis, lane-level rate lookups, rate comparisons across sources, alerting on rate changes, predictive pricing forecasts, and comprehensive rate analytics.
