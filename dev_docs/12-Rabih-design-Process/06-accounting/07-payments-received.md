## Screen Type

## Description
List of all customer payments received, including check, ACH, wire, and credit card payments. Shows payment date, amount, customer, applied invoices, and unapplied balances.

## Key Design Considerations
- Unapplied payments must be prominently highlighted for quick resolution
- Must support bulk payment application across multiple invoices

- Invoice records within this service
