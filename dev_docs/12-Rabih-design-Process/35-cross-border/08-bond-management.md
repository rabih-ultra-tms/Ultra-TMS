## Design Status

This screen will be designed as part of a future wave.

## Screen Type
List

## Description
Customs bond management interface showing active bonds, bond types (single entry, continuous), surety information, bond amounts, expiration dates, and utilization tracking.

## Key Design Considerations
- Bond expiration alerts with automatic renewal reminders and surety contact information
- Bond sufficiency calculator ensuring bond amounts are adequate for projected import volumes

## Dependencies
- Customs Entries (Service 35, Screen 2) for bond utilization tracking
- Document service for bond certificates
- Notification service for expiration and renewal alerts
