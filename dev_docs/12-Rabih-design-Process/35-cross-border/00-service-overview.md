# Service 35: Cross-Border

## Service Overview
The Cross-Border service manages international shipment compliance for loads crossing US-Canada and US-Mexico borders. It handles customs entries, broker management, document requirements, tariff classifications, bond management, and compliance verification to ensure smooth border crossings and regulatory adherence.
