## Description
Management interface for tracking reserve balances held by factoring companies, including reserve accruals, releases, adjustments, and projected release schedules.

## Key Design Considerations
- Reserve balance timeline showing accrual and release patterns over time
- Automated alerts when reserve balances exceed expected thresholds or release dates are missed

## Dependencies
- Remittance Tracking (Service 30, Screen 5)
- Factor company reserve policies from Factor Setup (Service 30, Screen 3)
- Accounting service for reserve balance reconciliation
