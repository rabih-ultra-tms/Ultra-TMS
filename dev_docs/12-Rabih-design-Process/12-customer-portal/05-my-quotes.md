## Description
Customer's quotes list showing all pending, accepted, and expired quotes with quote number, lane, rate, expiration date, and status. Supports accepting quotes directly from the portal.

## Key Design Considerations
- Quote expiration countdown creates urgency and encourages timely decisions
- One-click quote acceptance with confirmation streamlines the booking process
