## Description
Training program management interface showing available courses, employee training records, completion status, certification tracking, and mandatory training compliance.

## Key Design Considerations
- Compliance matrix showing which employees have completed required training vs. those who are overdue
- Training calendar with scheduling, enrollment capacity tracking, and automated reminder notifications

## Dependencies
- Employee data (Service 33, Screen 2)
- Safety service for DOT-required training records
- Notification service for training deadline reminders
- Document service for training certificates
