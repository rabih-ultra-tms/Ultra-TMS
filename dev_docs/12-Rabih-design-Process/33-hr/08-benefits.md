## Description
Benefits administration interface showing available benefit plans, employee enrollment status, coverage details, costs, and open enrollment management.

## Key Design Considerations
- Plan comparison tool allowing employees to compare coverage options, costs, and provider networks side by side
- Open enrollment workflow with deadline tracking and automated reminders for unenrolled employees

## Dependencies
- Employee data (Service 33, Screen 2)
- Payroll Overview (Service 33, Screen 7) for benefit deductions
- Document service for plan documents and enrollment forms
