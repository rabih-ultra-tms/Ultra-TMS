## Description
HR analytics and reporting interface providing insights on headcount trends, turnover rates, attendance patterns, training compliance, payroll costs, and workforce demographics.

## Key Design Considerations
- Pre-built report templates for common HR metrics (turnover, cost-per-hire, time-to-fill, training compliance)
- Exportable reports for regulatory compliance filings and executive workforce reviews
