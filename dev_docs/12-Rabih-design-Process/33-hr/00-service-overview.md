# Service 33: HR (Human Resources)

## Service Overview
The HR service manages all human resources functions within the TMS platform, including employee records, onboarding workflows, time and attendance tracking, leave management, payroll integration, benefits administration, and training programs. It provides a comprehensive people management solution for transportation companies.
