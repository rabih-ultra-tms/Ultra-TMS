## Design Status

## Screen Type

## Description
Leave request and approval management interface showing leave balances, pending requests, approval history, team calendar overlaps, and leave policy enforcement.

## Key Design Considerations
- Team calendar overlay showing who is already scheduled off to help managers make informed approval decisions
- Leave balance dashboard with accrual tracking and projected balance calculations

## Dependencies
- Employee data (Service 33, Screen 2)
- Time & Attendance (Service 33, Screen 5) for absence recording
- Notification service for leave request and approval alerts
