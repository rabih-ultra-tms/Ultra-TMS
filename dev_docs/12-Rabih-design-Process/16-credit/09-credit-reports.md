## Screen Type
Report

## Description
Credit analytics report showing total credit exposure, bad debt write-offs, credit application approval rates, average days to pay, collection effectiveness, and credit risk distribution across the customer portfolio.

## Key Design Considerations
- Bad debt trend analysis helps evaluate and refine the credit policy
- DSO (Days Sales Outstanding) tracking by customer segment identifies payment behavior patterns

## Dependencies

- Service 06 - Accounting (payment and write-off data)
- Service 18 - Analytics (shared reporting infrastructure)
