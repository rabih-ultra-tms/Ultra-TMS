## Description
Help articles library organized by category with search, browsing, and featured/popular article sections. Serves as the first line of self-service support.

## Key Design Considerations
- Full-text search with relevance ranking and category filtering
- Most-viewed and recently-updated article sections for quick access to popular content

## Dependencies
- Search service for article search functionality
- Content management for article creation and editing
- Analytics for article view tracking and usefulness ratings
